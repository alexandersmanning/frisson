Chisel editor

An writing app for cutting through text

View on GitHub Try demo
The sculpture is already complete within the marble block, before I start my work... I just have to chisel away the superfluous material. - Michaelangelo

I write in a disorganized manner. With a topic in mind, I generate tons of text, and then go back and read through it all, hunting for the good parts. I throw away 90% of what I write. Like Michaelangelo, I have a block of material in front of me, and I need to chisel away to the good parts. I wrote this editor to help me do just that.

Chisel helps you generate text

Expand, contract, rewrite, fix speech-to-text errors, fix passive voice. And it's easy to add more prompts.

Chisel helps you organize your text

Grid mode helps you organize your chapters. You can drag and drop chapters to move them around. Use the columns to define the beats of your story, and the rows for individual character arcs, alternate versions of chapters, or whatever you like.

Chisel keeps a history of your text, so you can undo and redo changes. It's like git for your writing; nothing you write is ever lost, even if you delete it.

Features

  • Built-in git-like history
  • Expand text, shorten, rewrite, and more
  • VS Code-like launcher for quick navigation
  • Dark mode
  • Grid mode
  • Easily add your own AI functionality
  • Support for multiple engines
